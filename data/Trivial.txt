TOPIC: iACADEMY GENERAL INFORMATION
Information and Communications Technology Academy, better known as iAcademy (stylized as iACADEMY), is a private, non-sectarian educational institution in the Philippines. The college offers specialized Senior High School and Undergraduate programs in fields relating to computer science, game development, multimedia arts, animation, and business management.


TOPIC: CAMPUS LOCATIONS
The college has two campuses:
1. iACADEMY Nexus in Yakal, located in the Makati Central Business District.
2. Cebu Campus, which was formally inaugurated in 2023.


TOPIC: HISTORY (2002 - 2009)
2002: iACADEMY was established.
2009: iACADEMY became an Authorized Training Partner of Wacom, a Japanese company that produces graphics tablets. It was also designated as the first IBM Center of Excellence in the ASEAN region.


TOPIC: HISTORY (2010 - PARTNERSHIPS)
In 2010, iACADEMY partnered with Runway Philippines Season 3 & 4. It also partnered with TV5 during the first automated elections in the Philippines. Together with DZRH, MBC, LENTE, Stratbase, ePLDT, and SWS, they worked on election coverage. In the same year, the college was appointed the first IBM Software Center of Excellence in the ASEAN Region and the first Lotus Academic Institute.


TOPIC: HISTORY (2011 - 2016)
2011: Chosen by Solar Entertainment Corporation as the official partner-school of Project Runway Philippines Season 3.
2015: Held the first 24-hour game competition called Battle League.
2016: Began offering specialized Senior High School programs.


TOPIC: HISTORY (2017 - GAME DEVELOPMENT)
2017: Began providing training and certification with UNITY as a Certified Training and Certification Partner. Became the official partner of the International Game Developers Association (IGDA) in hosting Manila Game Jam 2017.


TOPIC: HISTORY (2019 - TOON BOOM & PARTNERSHIPS)
2019: Partnered with PricewaterhouseCoopers for the Accountancy program. Recognized as the first and only Toon Boom Center of Excellence in Asia. Partnered with PythonPH to host PyCon APAC 2019. Became the first educational institution partner of the Department of Tourism (DOT) to develop a travel app and the "Maze" social distancing app.


TOPIC: HISTORY (2019 - SOCIAL INITIATIVES)
2019: Partnered with Museo ng Kaalamáng Katutubò (MusKKat) for indigenous artifact awareness. Partnered with Unilab to support community objectives. Partnered with the Climate Change Commission (CCC) for climate awareness exhibitions like CTRL+S Now.


TOPIC: HISTORY (2020 - 2023)
2020: Established partnership with Temasek Polytechnic in Singapore.
2022: Launched iACADEMY x ASUS Edukasyon partnership.
2023: Launched iNDIEGENIUS Project Lab supported by Netflix to support Filipino regional filmmakers.


TOPIC: INDUSTRY PARTNERSHIPS (2023)

TOPIC: SENIOR HIGH SCHOOL (SHS) STRANDS
iACADEMY offers three SHS tracks:
1. Academic Track: Accountancy Business and Management (ABM), Humanities and Social Sciences (HUMSS), and Robotics.
2. Technical-Vocational (Tech-Voc) Track: Computer Programming (Software Development), Animation, Fashion Design, and Graphic Illustration.
3. Arts and Design Track: Multimedia Arts and Audio Production.


TOPIC: SCHOOL OF COMPUTING (COLLEGE) (SOC)
The School of Computing is the first IBM Center of Excellence (CoE) in the ASEAN region and the official Microsoft Training Center in the Philippines.
Degrees Offered:
- BS Software Engineering
- BS Game Development
- BS Web Development
- BS Cloud Computing
- BS Data Science


TOPIC: SCHOOL OF BUSINESS AND LIBERAL ARTS (COLLEGE) (SBLA)
Degrees Offered:
- BS Marketing Management
- BS Real Estate Management (2nd Degree Fast Track Program)
- BA Psychology


TOPIC: SCHOOL OF DESIGN AND THE ARTS (COLLEGE) (SODA)
The School of Design and the Arts is the first to offer an Animation program in the Philippines and is the first Toon Boom Center of Excellence in Asia.
Degrees Offered:
- BS Animation
- BA Multimedia Arts and Design
- BA Fashion Design and Technology
- BA Film and Visual Effects
- BA Music Production and Sound Design


TOPIC: STUDENT LIFE AND CALENDAR
The Senior High School program follows a semester calendar (August to June).
The College program follows a trimester calendar (Starts in July).
SOAR (Student Orientation and Registration) is held a week before classes start to introduce students to the campus.


TOPIC: SCHOOL TRADITIONS AND EVENTS
- SOAR: Student Orientation and Registration for new students.
- Creative Camp: Free art workshops.
- Battle League: Gaming competition to promote the Game Development industry.
